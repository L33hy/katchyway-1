Katchyway Services


Get ready for an unforgettable experience as we take you on a thrilling ride down the rapids of some of the most beautiful rivers in the world. Our expert guides will ensure your safety while providing you with an adrenaline-pumping adventure that you'll never forget.

History

Founded in 1995, our company has been providing thrilling rafting adventures for over 25 years. Our founder, John Smith, was an avid outdoorsman who wanted to share his love of rafting with others. He started with just a few rafts and a small team of guides, but quickly grew the business as word spread about the incredible experiences we offer.

Over the years, we've expanded our operations to include trips on some of the most beautiful rivers in the world. We've also invested in top-of-the-line equipment and training for our guides to ensure that our customers have the safest and most enjoyable experience possible. We're proud of our history and the thousands of happy customers we've served over the years.
